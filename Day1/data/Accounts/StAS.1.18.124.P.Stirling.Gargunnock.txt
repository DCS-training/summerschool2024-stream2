   Valuation.-The valuation of the whole parish is L. 4127:
15: 2. Scottish money; but the real rental is above L. 3000
sterling.  About 30 years ago, it was only the half of that
sum.  There is now an increase at every term.  No farm is
now let without an additional rent.  Applications are made
for the farm, long before the lease expires.  The separate
estates, which comprehend the whole parish, are Leckie and
Kepdarroch, Boquhan, Gargunnock, Micklewood, Redhall,
and Culmore.
