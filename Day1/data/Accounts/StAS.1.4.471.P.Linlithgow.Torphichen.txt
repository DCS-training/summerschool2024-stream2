prudence, is at no difficulty to maintain and bring up his
children, if he keeps his health, along with the industry of
the mother.  The commonalty live on the produce of the
coutry, or what is called milk and meal, with a large
proportion of potatoes in winter.  The dress is greatly more
expensive than it was 30 years ago.
