   A regular town, upon an elegant plan, is already begun,
adjoining to the works, and there are houses built, sufficient
to accommodate 400 workers.  But the late deplorable de-
cay of trade, has thrown a damp upon this, and every other
scheme of manufacture; and they are proceeding, as slowly
as possible, in furnishing this extensive building.
