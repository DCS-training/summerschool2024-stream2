     NUMBER XXVI.

     PARISH OF ROSEMARKIE.

     (County and Synod of Ross-Presbytery of Chanonry.)

     By the Rev. Mr. ALEXANDER WOOD, Minister.

     Name and Extent.

   THE name of this parish was anciently spelled Rossmarkie,
and sometimes Rosemarknie.  The most probable ac-
count of the origin of the name is this: To the parish church,
in former times, was annexed a steeple, in an elevated and
conspicuous station, which being one of the first objects
observed by mariners in coming up the Murray Frith,
they would naturally say to one another, "Mark ye Ross!"
which, for the sake of better found, was turned into Ross-
markie.  The extent of the parish, from E. to W., is about
6 miles in length; and 3 miles in breadth, from S. to N.
