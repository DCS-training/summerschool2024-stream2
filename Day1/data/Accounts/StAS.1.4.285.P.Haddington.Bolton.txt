     NUMBER XXXVIII.

     PARISH OF BOLTON.

     (COUNTY OF HADDINGTON.)

     By the Rev. Mr JOHN HAMILTON.

     Situation and Surface.

   THE parish of Bolton is in the presbytery of Hadding-
ton, and Synod of Lothian and Tweeddale.  It is
very irregular in its boundaries and its dimensions; ex-
tending from the N. E. corner ot the S. W. near 6 miles;
and in breadth, at a medium, not above 1 mile and a
quarter; it is broadest at the two ends.  It is bounded by
Haddington parish on the N.; on the E. by that branch
of the Tyne running from Yester; on the S. by Yester
parish; on the W. by Humbie and Salton, which last in-
closes it on the N. W. corner.  There is a small rising,
with a gentle declivity on either side, running from E. to
W.
