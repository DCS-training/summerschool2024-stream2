   In the porch of the church, is a neat Monument, erected to the me-
mory of Mr Rolland, late of Gaik, father of Adam Rolland, Esq; Ad-
vocate, with the following excellent character of him, written in ele-
gant Latin.

     M. S.
     Adami Rolland de Gaik,
     Viri non uno nomine celebrandi,
     Utpote non paucis virtatibus ornati,
     Ob pietatem erga Deum,
     Amorem in patriam,
     Benevolentiam in genus humanture,
     Amabilis;
     Ob Vitae integritatem,
     Morum comitatem,
     Affectuum temperatiam,
     Spectabilis;
     Quisvos paterno, probos quosvis fraterno
     Omnes benigno animo amplexus;
     In publicis, privatisque officiis
     Prudens, fidus, diligens;
     Mente et manu munificus,
     Futurorum providus,
     Fortune semper securus:
     Ita volente
     D. O. M.
     XII. Calend. August M, DCC, XLIII.
     Etat. LVII.
     Animam Creatori, exuvias terrae,
     Reddidit;
     Trifte fui defiderium, amicis relinquents.

*Life and Acts of Robert Bruce by John Barbour.
