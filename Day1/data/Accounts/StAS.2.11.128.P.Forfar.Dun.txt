
128                        FORFARSHIRE.

	Inns, &c.--There is on the turnpike road a toll-house and an
alehouse, where spirituous liquors are sold; they are frequented
chiefly by the people travelling the turnpike road, but very seldom
by the people of this parish.
	Fuel.--English coal, procured at the shore in Montrose, from
1s. to 1s. 3d. per barrel, is the fuel generally used.

    January 1833.
