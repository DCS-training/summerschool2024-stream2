   School.-The schoolmaster's salary is 1 chalder, or 16 bolls
oat-meal.  The school here was formerly one of the most
flourishing in Caithness; but, for 12 years past, has had no
legally qualified teacher.  Mr Alexander Miller merchant in
Thurso, bestows 5 l. a year on a private school in this parish
for teaching poor children.
