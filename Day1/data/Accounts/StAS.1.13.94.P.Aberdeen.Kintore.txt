this parish are all covered with heath; whereas in Skene,

them; whereas hills, covered with heath, are sandy, and
the adjacent soil is warm and earlier, the air is more
kindly, and the grain on such grounds is richer, yield bet-
ter, and is of better quality; the country people say, it is
sharper and nearer the meal.
