   Wages.-A taylor's wages is from 4 d. to 6 d. per day, with
victuals.  Farm servants ger from 6 L. to 8 L. a year; house-
hold servants, nearly as much.  Cottagers, who work daily to
their master's, have, for wages, some land laid down with grass
for a cow or mare, or permission to feed with the farmer's
own cattle; their fuel led, with a bouse and yard free.
