In several places are tumuli, some of which clearly appear to
be the works of art.  there are likewise small inclosures of
turf artificially made, some of them square, and some circular.
Two always appear near each other; they are called in the
Gaelic, Reitagan.  Reite or Reitachas, in that language, sig-
nifies 'agreement;' and the tradition respecting them is,
that in former times, parties at variance met there with 
their friends for settling of differences.
