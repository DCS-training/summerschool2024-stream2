   Bridges and Roads-There are no bridges in the parish of
any note.  The roads are not good; they are made and re-
paired by the statute labour.  There are no turnpikes.

   Rent.-The rent of ground varies according to the soil.
The best inheld near the river, is from 15 s. to 20 s. per acre.
The outfield from 5 s. to 10 s. per acre.

   Fuel.-The fuel made use of for fire is chiefly turf.  Some
bring coals from Aberdeen; and a few tenants have peats
from the mosses in Fintray and Newmachar.
