
     Statistical Account

Better houses would encourage the tenantly to improve
their lands.  At present, from their coldness, their damp-
ness, and dirtiness, arise all those disorders which carry
them off before the period or nature's appointment.  The
elevated exposed sitution of a great part of the lands here
would require to be better sheltered.  Strips of planting
drawn across the high grounds would impart shelter and
warmth, and promote vegetation and fertility.
