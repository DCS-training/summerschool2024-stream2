     NUMBER XX.

     PARISH OF UDNY,

     (COUNTY OF ABERDEEN.)

     By the Rev. Mr. JOHN ROSE.

     Situation, Surface, Soil, and Minerals.

   THE parish of Udny was an erection from the neighbour-
ing parishes of Logie Buchan, Ellon, Tarves, and
Foveran, in the end of the 16th, or beginning of the 17th
century.  One estate pays stipend to the minister of Logie
Buchan, and five estates to the minister of Ellon; a full
evidence that these estates were taken off these two pa-
rishes.  It is situated in the county and Synod of Aberdeen,
and presbytery of Ellon.  In extent 5 computed miles
