meal at the market price, and fold it for 2d. or 3 d. the peck
lower to such as were on the poor's list, or had small families.

   Eminent Men.-This parish has given birth to two emi-
nent living characters, Dr John Witherspoon President of
the College of New Jersey, and Dr Charles Nisbet President
of the College of Carlisle, in America.
