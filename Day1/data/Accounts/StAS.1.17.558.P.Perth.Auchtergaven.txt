   A stratum of marl was also discovered, not long ago, in a
hollow piece of ground, upon Mr Stewart of Grandtully's
estate, but it is buried under a great depth of moss.
   Mr Robertson of Tullybelton had the merit of introducing
into this parish the new method of farming by summer fal-
lowing, by a regular rotation of white and green crops, and
sowing grass seeds.
   Except one or two Druidical circles of large stones stand-
ing on end, similar to those that are to be seen in many
other parts of Scotland, there are no antiquities here worth
mentioning.
