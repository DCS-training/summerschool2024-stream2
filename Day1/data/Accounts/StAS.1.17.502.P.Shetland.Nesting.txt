   Conclusion.-It is hoped that the account of the minister
of the parish will render it unnecessary to make any use of
this imperfect sketch, which is only wrote in a hurry from
mere memory, by a person who resided there, in a public

with patience, that there should be any blank in the Statistical
Account of Scotland, if it was in his power to supply it.
