
		  PARISH OF LETHNOT AND NAVAR.

	PRESBYTERY OF BRECHIN, SYNOD OF ANGUS AND MEARNS.

       	   THE REV. ALEXANDER GARDNER, A. M. MINISTER.

            I.--TOPOGRAPHY AND NATURAL HISTORY.
   LETHNOT and Navar, formerly separate parishes, were united in
1723.
   Extent, &c.--The extent of the parish in length, from north-west
to south-east, is about 15 miles; in average breadth about 5 miles.
