   The most obvious and important improvements to be made
in this parish are these two; the introduction of manufac-
tures, and dividing and inclosing the land with hedge-rows
and belts of planting; the former would give a better direc-
tion to the industry of the inhabitants, and greatly meliorate
their condition; the latter would at once beautify and im-
prove the country.
