   It is not to be supposed that every little district should
possess any striking peculiarity of character; and to touch
the nice discriminating shades, is neither easy in itself, nor
necessary in a work of this nature.  The following fact places
the character of the people of this parish in no unfavourable
light:  Within the memory of man, there has been no in-
stance of suicide, nor of any criminal prosecution against an
inhabitant of Auchindoir.
