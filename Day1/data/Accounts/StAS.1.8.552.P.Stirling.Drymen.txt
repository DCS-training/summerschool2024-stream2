
     Statistical Account

   Eminent Men.-This parish is said to have been the birth
place, and at times the residence of the famous NAPIER of
MERCHISTON, the celebrated inventor of the Logarithms.

   Antiquities.-The only mark of antiquity, now to be
discerned, is a large earthen mound, at a place called
Cashlie, near which, it is said, the vestiges of a Roman
Camp were formerly to be seen, though now no traces of
it are to be found.
