some other remarkable event.  The mosses and moors in the
parish supply the inhabitants with fuel; but some coal is
brought from a distance.  The people, accustomed to the
pastoral life in their early years, are rather inclined to indo-
lence and ease.  Their condition might be improved by the
introduction of manufacturers.
