
     Statistical Account

   Ecclesiastical State.-The minister's stipend is 9 chalders
of victual, and 600 merks Scots, or L. 33: 6: 8 Sterling in
money.  Mr Gordon of Buthlaw is patron.  The church
was built in 1787.  The old church had stood since 1608,
at which period the parish-church was removed from St
Colm to Lonmay.
