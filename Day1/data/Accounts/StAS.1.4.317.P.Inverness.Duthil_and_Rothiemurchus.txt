approach of an enemy, the signal was sent from the one to
the other, for all fit to bear arms to appear at an appoint-
ted place.  Hence the Grants motto, 'Stand fast Craig-
'Elachie.'-The only principal inn in the parish is at A-
viemore.  There are no ale-houses.  The number of houses
in which whisky (a beverage which seems fit only for dae-
mons) is sold, is 10.  There were many more, until of late,
when they were suppressed by the proprietor, upon find-
ing the very bad effects on the morals of the people.
