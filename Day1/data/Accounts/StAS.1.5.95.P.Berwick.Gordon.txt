   The manner of livings, dress, and manners of the people
are greatly improved, within these last 20 years. Learning, in
particular, is more generally dissused than formerly, in proof
of which it may be remarked, that several of the farmers
here, have become subscribers to the public libraries in the
neighbouring town of Kelso, by which they are enabled to
acquire an extent of knowledge and information, to which,
otherwise, they could not have had access.
