     Origin of the Name.

   THE ancient and modern name of this parish, as far as
can now be ascertained, is that of Wick, an appella-
tion common all over the northern continent of Europe,
supposed to signify the same with the Latin word vicus, a
village or small town, particularly when lying adjacent to a
bay, or arm of the sea, resembling a wicket.  When any
