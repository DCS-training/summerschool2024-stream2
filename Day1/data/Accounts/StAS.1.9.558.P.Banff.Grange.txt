
     Statistical Account

No register of deaths or burials was ever kept here till, in
1783, an act of Parliament was passed, imposing a duty up-
on the registration of baptisms, marriages, and burials; af-
ter which, a register of burials was attempted to be kept,
but the tax was so odious and unpopular, (being considered
by the majority of the people as a tax upon their misfor-
tunes), that it could not be collected, but with great diffi-
culty; at the same time, that it afforded little information
