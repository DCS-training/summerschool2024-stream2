     NUMBER XXV.

     PARISH OF MADDERTY,

     (COUNTY OF PERTH, SYNOD OF PERTH AND STIRLING,
     PRESBYTERY OF AUCHTERARDER.)

     By a Friend to Statistical Inquiries.

     Situation, etc.

   THIS parish is situated on the western boundaries of the
parishes of Gask and Trinity-Gask, and on the east-
ern of Criess.  It has Fowils on the north, and part of Tri-
nity-Gask on the south.-The climate is considerably wet
and cold.  The low and damp lands alongst the banks of
the water of Pow are believed to be productive of rheuma-
tisms, a disease of which the inhabitants of that district fre-
quently complain.
