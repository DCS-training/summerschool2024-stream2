Another improvement to be recommended is, that of convert-
ing the farms, from a cattle to a sheep stock, for which this
parish is particularly well calculated.  It would be unnecessary
to remove the present possessors for that purpose; for unless
they are very stuborn and obstinate indeed, they might easily
be prevailed upon to convert their cattle into sheep; and to
learn the best modes of managing the new stock, in the acquir-
ing of which, there is no difficulty or witchcraft.
