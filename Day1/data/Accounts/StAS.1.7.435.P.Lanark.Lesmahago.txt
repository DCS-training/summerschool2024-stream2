proposed to be brought into Parliament for making a road
from Lanark to Hamilton, to pass through this parish, along
the banks of the Clyde, to wind along in a level course,
shunning all sudden risings and declivities, which, when ex-
ecuted, will not only greatly promote the internal intercourse
through this part of the country, but entertain the passenger
with a view of the most majestic natural scenery.
