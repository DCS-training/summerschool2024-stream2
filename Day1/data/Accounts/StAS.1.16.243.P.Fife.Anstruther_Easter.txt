     PARISH OF ANSTRUTHER EASTER,

     (COUNTY AND SYNOD OF FIFE, PRESBYTERY OF
     ST ANDREW'S).

     By a Friend to Statistical Inquiries.

     Situation, etc.

   THIS parish is situated on the sea-coast, between Kil-
rennie and Anstruther Wester, (from which it is di-
vided by a small river), in the Presbytery of St Andrew's.
   The observations that occur in the Statistical Account
of these two parishes, as to the soil, climate, fishing, price
of provisions, equally apply to Anstruther Easter, and need
not be repeated here.
