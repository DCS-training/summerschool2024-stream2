A small library was established last year for the use of the
parish.  The number of volumes, as yet, does not exceed
200.  The increase, in a great measure, depends on the coun-
tenance and support of the more opulent ranks.  The Sta-
tistical Accounts so far as published constitutes part of the
collection.
