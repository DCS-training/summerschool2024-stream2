   This parish has several advantages.  It is near markets,
for disposing of grain, cattle, or stock on the farms, being
7 miles from Berwick and 2 from Eyemouth.  It is at no
great distance from caol:  The price, however, considering
that distance, is certainly high.  The post road to London
goes through the parish:  And the vicinity of the sea affords
a good supply of most kinds of fish.  The parish of Ayton
does much more than supply itself with provisions.
