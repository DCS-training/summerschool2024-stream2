
     Statistical Account

     NUMBER XIX.

     UNITED PARISHES

     OF

     WHITSOM AND HILTON,

     (COUNTY OF BERWICK, SYNOD OF MERSE AND TEVIOT-
     DALE, AND PRESBYTERY OF CHURNSIDE),

     By the Rev. Mr GEORGE CUPPLES, Minister of Swinton.

     Situation, etc.

   THE united parishes of Whitsom and Hilton, in the
presbytery of Chirnside, Berwickshire, are conjectu-
red to extend in length from W. to E. about 4 1/2 miles
English; from S. to N. the breadth does not exceed 2 1/2 miles.
They are bounded by Edrom parish on the W. and on
the N.; by the parish of Hutton on the E; and by Swin-
ton and Ladykirk on the S.
