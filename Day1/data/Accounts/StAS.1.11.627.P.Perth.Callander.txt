   BRITANNIA sits already on a solitary rock, hangs down her
head, and, with her eyes bent towards AMERICA, she de-
plores the departure of her sons.  Ere long, file she shall shed
flood of tears, and her cries of distress will be heard in vain;
when her lion is trodden in the mire, by his foes: and none to
afford relief.
