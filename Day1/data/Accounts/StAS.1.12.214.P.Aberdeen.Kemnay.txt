
     Statistical Account

The Schoolmaster's living, by the account of the present in-
cumbent, including the parochial salary, the interest of some
legacies, school-fees, and all other emoluments, does not, one
year with another, exceed 8 L.  Sterling a-year.

<FOOTNOTE>

Aberdeen.  The poor got some part of the supply that was given by Government
to the northern counties; and the kirk-session bought some grain, which
was sold to the poor considerably below prune cost.  No person died of want.
