
     Statistical Account
In 1700, hops were not used in brewing ale.
In 1792, 15 cwt. hops were imported.
In 1700, a good cow was bought for 8 s. or 10 s.
In 1794, a good cow sells for L. 2.
In 1700, the wages of a maid-servant was 5 s. a-year.
In 1794, the wages of a maid-servant is 15 s. or L. 1 a-
   year.
In 1700, no dancing-school had ever been in these pa-
   rishes.
In 1793, a dancing-master opened a school, obtained 40 or
   50 scholars, and drew L. 50 in four months.
